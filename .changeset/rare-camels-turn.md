Add `rh-accordion`

An element which can be used to display accordions inside of an app.  

```html
<rh-accordion>
    <rh-accordion-header>
        <h2>Item One</h2>
    </rh-accordion-header>
    <rh-accordion-panel>
        Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et
        dolore magna
        aliqua.
    </rh-accordion-panel>
    <rh-accordion-header>
        <h2>Item Two</h2>
    </rh-accordion-header>
    <rh-accordion-panel>
        Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et
        dolore magna
        aliqua.
    </rh-accordion-panel>
    <rh-accordion-header>
        <h2>Item Three</h2>
    </rh-accordion-header>
    <rh-accordion-panel>
        Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et
        dolore magna
        aliqua.
    </rh-accordion-panel>
</rh-accordion>
```
