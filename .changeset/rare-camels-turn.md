Added `rh-accordion`, An element used to display multiple, related disclosure 
widgets.

```html
<rh-accordion>
  <rh-accordion-header>
    <h2>Item One</h2>
  </rh-accordion-header>
  <rh-accordion-panel>
    <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod 
    tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</p> 
    </rh-accordion-panel>
  <rh-accordion-header>
    <h2>Item Two</h2>
  </rh-accordion-header>
  <rh-accordion-panel>
    <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod 
    tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</p>
  </rh-accordion-panel>
  <rh-accordion-header>
    <h2>Item Three</h2>
  </rh-accordion-header>
  <rh-accordion-panel>
    <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod 
    tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.</p>
  </rh-accordion-panel>
</rh-accordion>
```
