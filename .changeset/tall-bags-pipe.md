`<rh-badge>`: added new states: `danger`, `warning`, `caution`, `neutral`, and 
`info`.

We deprecated:

  * `critical` (now an alias to `danger`)
  * `important` (now an alias to `warning`)
  * `moderate` (now an alias to `caution`)
  * `note` (now an alias to `info`)
