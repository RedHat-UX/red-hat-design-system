`<rh-badge>`: added new states: 

  * `danger`
  * `warning`
  * `caution`
  * `neutral`
  * `info`

We deprecated:

  * `critical` (now an alias to `danger`)
  * `important` (now an alias to `caution`)
  * `moderate` (now an alias to `warning`)
  * `note` (now an alias to `info`)
