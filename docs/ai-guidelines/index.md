## About AI guidelines

These guidelines cover how to design and build AI-enabled experiences that are consistent with the Red Hat brand and design language. They are maintained by the [Project Felt](https://github.com/project-felt/ai-guidelines) team and shared across Red Hat digital properties including ux.redhat.com and patternfly.org.
