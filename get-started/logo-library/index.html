Product logo library

Overview

Our product logos connect every new product or service to our brand. Their consistent format allows us to add new product logos to our portfolio without additional design or legal costs. This library includes the following product logos. To see a repo of all logos, visit this page.

  • Standard product logos
  • Product logo variants

Warning
Not all product logos include a variant. Use what is available, do not create your own variants.

Get started

Available product logos can be accessed in the Libraries panel in Adobe XD. You can search for a logo by name or by expanding any corresponding section.

Adobe XD

This library is maintained in Adobe XD and Adobe Creative Cloud. They need to be installed if you want access to product logos. Once a license is granted, Associates can install their desired app(s) from within Adobe Creative Cloud. Contact your manager for further assistance if necessary.

Request a Creative Cloud license Learn how to use libraries

Usage

Product logos

Use product logos on websites or other web properties, their recommended size is at least 48px tall to maintain legibility.

Component states

Use Component States in XD to toggle between light theme (regular) and dark theme (reverse) product logos.

Warning
Resizing a product logo and switching component states will revert the logo to the default size. Select a component state and then resize.

Sizing

When product logos with short and long names are the same height, some will appear smaller or larger than others.

Multiple logos

If you use product logos with short and long names in close proximity to each other, adjust the height so the hat icons are the same size.

Best practices

Inconsistent sizing

Do not use product logos at the same height in close proximity to each other.

One-color versions

Do not use the one-color version of any product logo or the Red Hat logo on any web property.

More information

For more information about product logo best practices, visit this page.

Request an invite

Ensure that you have XD installed and your Adobe account is in order before requesting an invite. When ready, contact us and tell us about you and what you are working on in relation to this library.

Frequently asked questions

When are new product logos added to this library?
New brand assets are released every month, so any new product logos will be added within one day.

How do I request a new product logo?
To request a new product logo, contact the Brand team.

How do I request an existing product logo be added to this library?
If your desired product logo is not found in this library, check if it exists in the product logo repo first. If the product logo does exist in that repo but not in this library, contact the Design system team and it will be added within one day.

Why is a product logo no longer available?
It might have been deprecated depending on the status of the product. To find out more, contact the Brand team for missing product logos.
