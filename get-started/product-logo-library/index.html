Product logo library

Introduction

Our product logos connect every new product or service to our parent brand, Red Hat. Their consistent format allows us to add new product logos to our portfolio without additional design or legal costs. Visit this page to see our repo of logos.

Using libraries

Our libraries require access to Adobe Creative Cloud through a Red Hat account. These libraries are intended for designers who regularly use Adobe CC apps as part of their role at Red Hat. Access will not be granted to accounts that are not part of the Red Hat domain.

Request a Creative Cloud license

Request access

To request access to the Adobe CC libraries containing brand assets, fill out the Brand CC libraries request form. Please allow a week or less to receive access.

When your request is accepted, you will be invited to join the Logo and color palette library, the Product logos and icons library, and the Standard and UI icons library. These libraries will then sync to your Adobe CC design programs and you can view the assets in the Libraries panel.

Types of product logos

Every product logo starts with the Red Hat logo and follows with the full product name. If the product has more than one version under a common product name, a third line is added in a lighter font weight.

Warning
Not all product logos include a variant. Use what is available, do not create your own variants.

Standard product logo

Product logo with variant

Product logo colors

Red Hat product logos are produced in two full-color versions: standard and reverse. They are also produced in two one-color versions: white and black.

Product logo sizing

Product logo names with different line counts will appear larger or smaller when they are the same height. To fix this, adjust the height of each product logo manually so the fedora icons are all the same height.

Best practices

Inconsistent sizing

Do not assign product logos the same height value, adjust the height of each product logo manually so the fedora icons are all the same height.

One-color product logos

Do not use the one-color version of any product logo or the Red Hat logo on any web property.

Frequently asked questions

When are the libraries updated with new product logos?
New assets are added about once per month, however, sometimes product logos are not included in that release. Updates to our libraries are communicated over e-mail and if you want to be added to the distribution list, contact the Brand team.

Can I request a new product logo?
To request a new product logo, contact the Brand team.

I do not see my desired product logo in the library, how do I find it?
If you are having trouble locating a product logo in the library, contact the Brand team.
